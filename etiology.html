Etiology

  • Infants: Staphylococcus aureus, Streptococcus agalactiae, Escherichia coli
  • Children over 1 year: Staphylococcus aureus, Streptococcus pyogenes, Hemophilus influenzae
  • Hematogenous: Single organism
  • Contiguous focus: Multiple organisms often present

Staphylococcus aureus is the most frequently isolated pathogen. Gram-negative and anaerobic bacteria are also common.
